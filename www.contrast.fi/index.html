What our clients say:

For the user interface of our professional photo and media management software we had two requirements. On the one hand we were looking for a company that was able to deliver a clean and modern interface design. On the other hand we needed support by optimizing the user experience of our software and the developer driven workflows. Contrast was the perfect partner for both tasks. We had the pleasure to work with experienced interface and workflow designers, who delivered creative drafts and perfect final versions. Their individual templates allowed our developers to implement everything easily and quickly. Despite of the distance from Munich to Helsinki, the communication between contrast and us was very fertile and target-aimed. We love to work with contrast and we are looking forward to every new project with this professional and dynamic agency. Richard Michel Pixx.io
The Finnish Ski Area Association chose Contrast as their partner to rebrand and renew their Ski.fi ski resort portal. Project wasn't too easy, it included visual rebranding, remake of the website and transfer onto another CMS and complete renewal of the mobile site. The schedule was also really tight. Despite all this, the final result exceeds expectations. The project stayed on schedule and collaboration with Contrast has been effortless and pleasant. Naturally, our collaboration will continue in the form of updates on the site, but we are also planning new projects with Contrast. Open communication, great attitude and professionalism combined with creativity as well as the ambition to do things properly will likely propel Contrast onto a great career! We can sincerely recommend! Anne Larilahti Ski.fi
The Helsinki City Museum will open a new Museum at the corner of Senate Square in May 2016. We needed a website, through which the public could follow the project and it's development. We at the museum, as well as our customers have all been very pleased with the site Contrast Digital Agency designed and built for us. Updating the site is effortless and the site looks stylish. During the design process, our wishes were carefully taken into account, and Contrast team was very flexible and helpful throughout the process. Even long after the completion, we have always gotten help whenever we have needed it. Reeta Holma Helsinki City Museum
